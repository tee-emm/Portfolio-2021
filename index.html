02. ABOUT ME

Hey there! Thanks for checking out my website. I am a current Global Business and Digital Arts student at the University of Waterloo. I believe design is all about creating (fun)ctional solutions to every day problems in an aesthetically pleasing manner. I want to improve the relationships between people and the products they use in their everyday life by using innovative design solutions to create meaningful user experiences.

Most of all, I'm a storyteller. The real world is sometimes far too complex, and so I like to give it form.

I don't mind if you love to know more about me, check me out on Instagram and LinkedIn.


SUDOKU
The classic logic-based puzzle game, but a little bit prettier.

Sudoku (Go to Site)

For my final project in my beginner programming course, I decided to code the classic puzzle game of Sudoku. Sudoku is a logic-based, number-placement puzzle. The objective of the game is to fill a 9x9 grid board with digits so that every 3x3 box (subgrid), column, and row in the grid contain all of the digits from 1 - 9. Each digit can only appear once in each subdivision (column, row, or box). My program is able to auto-solve and regenerate new Sudoku boards. Additionally, my program gives players the option to save and load their game states (current grids) so that they can enjoy the game at their own leisure.


IDIOMATIC
Travel with Princess Pinky and Collect all the Idiom Gems!

Idiomatic (Go to Site)

A side-scrolling pixel game that I created for my Colour Theory course as my capstone project. Travel with Pinky the Princess throughout all the different worlds to collect all of the idiom gems. Click on the different characters to learn more about each idiom and collect additional gems.
